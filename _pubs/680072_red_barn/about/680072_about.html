The Red Barn is a fantastic traditional pub that you would be proud to call your local.

Situated on Longden Road just outside Shrewsbury town centre, the Cask Marque accredited Red Barn is right at the heart of the local community.

The Red Barn is family-friendly with something for everyone. A place where you’re always welcome.

From the kitchen, you can enjoy a delicious seasonal menu with many dishes to choose from. Our Sunday roasts alone are worth popping in for! From the bar, enjoy one of our premium lagers, cask ales or refreshing gin & tonics! There’s lots for you to choose from.

If it’s live sport you’re after, we’ve got that covered too! Our TV screens will be showing all the biggest matches on Sky Sports and BT Sport. See whats on

In the warmer months, soak up the rays in our large beer garden. It’s the perfect spot for spending time with the family!
