The Nottingham Arms is a fantastic traditional pub that you would be proud to call your local.

Situated on the high street of the historic riverside town of Tewkesbury the Nottingham Arms is known for its quality food and vibrant atmosphere.

It’s a true town centre pub that’s family-friendly with something for everyone.

Behind the bar we’ve got everything from real ales to wines, soft drinks to gins and more. From the kitchen, you can enjoy a delicious seasonal menu with many dishes to choose from. Our Sunday roasts alone are worth popping in for! From the bar, enjoy one of our premium lagers, cask ales or refreshing gin & tonics! There’s lots for you to choose from.

The Nottingham Arms is the ideal place to kick back and enjoy a pint right in the heart of the medieval town of Tewkesbury.
