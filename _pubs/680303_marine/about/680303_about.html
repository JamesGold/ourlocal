For those warmer days, why not kick back and enjoy time in the gardens with your whole family - yes! Dogs are welcome in the front beer garden, while the kids play in our brand new outdoor play area, they can challenge you to a game of table tennis or football table, you will be able to sit back, relax and take in the sport on our outdoor TV’s, as well as enjoying a sumptuous dish from our menus.

At the Marine Bar & Kitchen, we serve food every day! From Monday to Friday we serve food 12–9pm, then 10:30 -8pm on a Saturday and Sunday. Our menus are bursting with choices and flavours! Check them out on our food page. Dining out at the Marine Bar & Kitchen is a great way to spend time with friends and family whatever the occasion. We’ve got your traditional pub favourites, mouth-watering flatbreads, and even vegan options such as our Cambodian street food curry.

We look forward to seeing you soon
