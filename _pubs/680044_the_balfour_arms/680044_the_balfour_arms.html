A pub you will be proud to call your local.

Somewhere you’re always welcome and relaxed, whether by yourself, or with friends or family. At the heart of the community, it’s everything a village pub should be. Delicious wholesome pub food and a wide selection of hot and cold drinks served by friendly staff.

We love to give plenty of reasons to keep visiting us so we ensure we’re always bringing interesting new lines into our cask and craft ranges on the bar. Our great menu wouldn’t be complete without the right drinks to enjoy so we have an extensive range of wines & spirits.
