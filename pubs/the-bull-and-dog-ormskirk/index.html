The Bull & Dog in Burscough is a proper local pub, right at the heart of the local community. The pub retains all of its charm & warmth of welcome for both regulars and visitors alike.

Located on the main road through Burscough, this impressive old inn offers something for everyone. Feeling a little hungry, or just fancy a quick pint? Drop by and join us. Find out more about the Bull & Dog, Burscough, Ormskirk.
